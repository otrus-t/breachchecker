Check If Your Passwords Are Safe Online Or Not.

Hackers steal millions of passwords from big companies every year and post them online. This tool tells you — in plain English — whether yours is already out there, and how to fix it. It is free, and it does not save anything.

Check Your Email Address

When a company gets hacked, your email and password can end up in a leaked file that gets shared online. Enter your email and we will show you exactly which companies leaked your details, when it happened, and what was taken.

One thing to know: to look up your email, it has to be sent to a breach-checking service (XposedOrNot). Only use your own address. We don't store anything on this page.
